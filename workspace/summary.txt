**Summary:**

A video captured moments before the death of Quentin Beck, also known as Mysterio, in London. Beck was attacked by Spider-Man, who used advanced Stark technology and an army of weaponized drones. The E.D.I.T.H. (Electronic Defense Infrastructure Tactical Headquarters) advised against the attack due to potential casualties but it was executed.

The Daily Bugle.net reported on this incident, claiming that Spider-Man was responsible for Mysterio's murder. They emphasized Mysterio's heroic efforts and suggested he would be remembered as one of the greatest superheroes. However, they hinted at more shocking revelations to come.
